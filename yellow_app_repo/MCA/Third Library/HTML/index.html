Frequently Asked Questions

01 Can I use Yellow App if I am not part of a community?
Yes you can.
02 Is there a registration fee for communities?
Registration for communities is free.
03 Is there an eligibility criterion for communities?
Yes there is. There should be a minimum of 15 flats in a community to be eligible for Yellow App.
04 Are discounts, deals and offers available for free?
Discounts, deals & offers are only free for the first three months.
05 Can I List my business in the services section?
Yes you can. You can get in touch with our Vendor Management team for the same.
06 Can I provide daily deals and offers for my business?
You can provide daily deals & offers only after paying a minimal subscription fee to Yellow App.
